Gulab jamun 

For Jamun:
1. ¾ cup (100 grams) milk powder, unsweetened
2. ½ cup (60 grams) maida
3. ½ tsp baking powder
4. 2 tbsp ghee 
5. milk 
6. ghee or oil 

For Sugar Syrup :
1. 2 cup sugar
2. 2 cup water
3. 2 cardamom
4. ¼ tsp saffron / kesar
5. 1 tsp lemon juice
6. 1 tsp rose water
